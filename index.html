Marvel Heroes(Avengers)

Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange, whose full name is Stephen Strange, is the mystical powerhouse of the Avengers and one of the most powerful sorcerers in the Marvel Universe.

Iron Man

Iron Man, whose real name is Tony Stark, is one of the most iconic members of the Avengers. He’s not a superhero because of superpowers, but because of his genius-level intellect, unmatched engineering skills, and an unshakable determination to protect others.

Captain America

Captain America, whose real name is Steve Rogers, is one of the most beloved and enduring heroes of the Marvel Universe.Struggling to adjust to a world that had changed beyond recognition, Captain America found his place again as the leader of the Avengers,

Thor

Thor, in the Marvel Universe, is both a superhero and a god — drawn from Norse mythology but reimagined as one of Marvel’s most powerful and noble characters. He is the God of Thunder, the son of Odin (All-Father of Asgard) and Gaea (the Earth goddess), making him both an Asgardian and connected to Midgard (Earth) in a unique way

Hawkeye

Hawkeye, whose real name is Clint Barton, is one of the most skilled marksmen in the Marvel Universe and a long-time member of the Avengers. Unlike many of his teammates, Clint doesn’t have superhuman powers — his strength lies in his incredible archery skills, razor-sharp aim, and quick thinking under pressure.

Natasha

Natasha Romanoff, better known as Black Widow, is one of the most skilled and mysterious members of the Avengers. She’s a master spy, assassin, and hand-to-hand combat expert, with a history that’s as shadowy as it is fascinating.

Black Panther

Black Panther, whose real name is T’Challa, is a Marvel superhero and a member of the Avengers, known for his unmatched combat skills, regal presence, and deep sense of responsibility. He’s not just a hero — he’s also the king of Wakanda, a fictional African nation that’s the most technologically advanced country in the Marvel Universe, thanks to its vast reserves of vibranium, a rare, powerful metal.

Spider Man

Spider-Man is one of Marvel’s most beloved and relatable superheroes — a character who balances high-flying heroics with the everyday struggles of being a young adult.His real name is Peter Parker, a bright but often awkward teenager from Queens, New York. His life changes forever when he’s bitten by a radioactive spider
